How the Unit Converter Works

Our unit converter uses standardized conversion factors based on international standards. The tool supports bidirectional conversions, meaning you can convert from any supported unit to any other supported unit within the same category.

For length conversions, the tool uses the meter as the base unit, converting all measurements to meters internally before converting to the target unit. This ensures maximum precision and accuracy across all supported units.

Understanding Measurement Systems

The world uses two primary measurement systems:

  • Metric System (SI): Used by most countries worldwide, based on powers of 10
  • Imperial/US Customary: Used primarily in the United States, based on historical measurements

Our converter bridges these systems seamlessly, making it easy to work with measurements from any source.

Precision and Accuracy

All conversions use official conversion factors:

  • 1 meter = 3.28084 feet
  • 1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds
  • 1 liter = 0.264172 gallons
  • 1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles

These factors are based on international standards and provide results accurate to within 0.0001%.
